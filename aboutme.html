About me


Download
What can I offer?

Strong communication: I think it is important for game designers to document their vision of the game as precisely as possible, to facilitate collaboration with those who have to work with it. I also value feedback, both giving and receiving, as it serves a vital role in iteratively improving the game.

Multidisciplinary: With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and seven years of programming experience, I can work together closely with engineers.

Passion: I have experienced many unforgettable moments through games, and my dream is to be able to design such experiences myself.

Team player: Game design involves a lot of teamwork, hence I always aim to put the team first; a good environment leads to good games.

What kind of games?

While I enjoy any type of game, I especially like multiplayer games where social interaction is part of the game. MMORPGs, Boardgames, multiplayer-rpgs, co-op games, competitive games and so on. I find the dynamics of integrating social interaction into a game extremely interesting and a powerful tool in designing.
