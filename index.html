Helloo! I'm a game designer and programmer driven by a lifelong fascination with the fantasy worlds we call games. After studying Computer Science in Antwerp I pursued a master's in Game Studies & Engineering in Austria to learn and research about what games can mean for people and society.

I am very passionate about exploring what games can mean to people and society. Designing games is not just about mechanics or systems, but about shaping experiences that influence how people feel, think, and interact with the world.
